Volunteer FAQs

Who are we?

At INCO Academy Ireland, we provide fully funded tech courses to underserved communities. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and support diversity in tech.

With this in mind, we don't just focus on hard skills, we provide soft skills support including individual coaching on the topics of how to write a brilliant CV and practice interviews.

Who are our students?

  • 95% Under the age of 35 years old
  • 87% Graduation Rate
  • 75% Womxn

How does a volunteer help?

We all know how important it is to ask a friend to look over a CV or help you prep for interviews. Especially when you're trying to enter a new industry! We want you to be that friend for our students.

We need volunteers who work in tech to help us coach our students in CV writing and mock interview workshops.

You don't have to be a HR professional - this is the insider knowledge you'd offer a friend if they were applying for a role in your general industry.

We need your help proof reading English, giving pointers on how to phrase non-typical career experiences and answering interview questions in an online environment.

Your experiences in the digital sector are incredibly valuable to our students who don't have access to such expertise otherwise.
