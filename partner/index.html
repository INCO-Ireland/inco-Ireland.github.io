Who are we?

At INCO Academy Ireland, we provide fully funded tech courses to underserved communities. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and support diversity in tech.

The Employer Partner Network

If you have been following the INCO Academy in Ireland, you know that we have been working hard on supporting our students to obtain new skills and boost their employability.

We are now actively working with employers to create more pathways for our graduates.

Why should I join?

Diverse workforces are more creative, productive and profitable.

Promote upcoming opportunities in your business to our student body; a diverse group of highly qualified individuals with key skills and know-how.

We know the importance of a well-developed skillset.

Our graduates have completed either the one-month Get Into Tech bootcamp or an advanced coding course.

Through our network, you can learn more about the skillsets you can expect to find in our alumni body and gain the insight required to consider our candidates for your next open role.
